Crossroads Classic Analytics Challenge (CCAC) 2025

Introduction

What is CCAC 2025?

The Crossroads Classic Analytics Challenge (CCAC) is an annual competition where undergraduate and graduate student teams solve a data-driven business case. In 2025, our sponsor, the NCAA, is interested in how school affinity influences predictions in March Madness brackets. Each team will build predictive models and create a business proposal to showcase their findings.

Key Sponsor: NCAA

Focus: March Madness bracket submissions & school affinity

Team Size: 4–5 students (Undergrad and Grad compete separately)

Registration Fee: $40 per team

Important Platforms:

  • CCAC Website: Main hub for rules, timelines, and resources
  • Pitch: Communication platform to stay in touch with organizers (access provided upon registration)
  • Kaggle: Submit predictions and track progress on the live leaderboard
  • Gradescope: Submit Milestone 1 (Google doc), Milestone 2 (Workshops attendance), Milstone 3 (5 min Video)

Milestone 1: Team Preparation (20pts)

Form Your Team

  • Recommended team size: 4–5 students
  • Appoint a Team Captain
  • Decide on a unique team name
  • Set Up a Shared Google Doc:
    1. Title it “[Team Name] – CCAC 2025”
    2. List team members, roles, and contact info
    3. Include everyone's availability / meeting times

Register Your Team

  • Team Captain go the CCAC website
  • Completes the registration form (team name, members’ names, RHIT emails)
  • Pays the $40 team fee
  • All team members will receive instructions for:
    • Signing the NDA (required for Kaggle access)
    • Joining Pitch for official competition communications

What is NDA

  1. You cannot share the datasets outside your team. That includes the use of public AI tools that train on submitted data.
  2. Once the competition is complete, the datasets must be deleted.
  3. All code and dashboards must be turned in to the NCAA and become the property of the NCAA to be used as they see fit.
  4. Note: teams will be expelled for using genAI tools.
